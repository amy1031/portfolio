About


I grew up in California on an almond farm. When I wasn't outside riding my bike or climbing trees, I was inside playing around on the computer. In junior high I discovered Geocities and spent time discovering how to change backgrounds, colors, and add images. In high school I found out about Wordpress (back when it was nothing more than 6 files) and spent my free time figuring out how modify it. Beyond high school, making websites continued to be a hobby. Fast forward to when I worked at the Idaho State Department of Education: I helped the Web Manager re-do the website. I found my pasison in web development again and decided to pursue it.
